Crystal Collector

Here are the rules to the game!

  1. You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

  2. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  3. To win the game, you must match your total score to the random number.

  4. If you go above the random number, you lose the game.

  5. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

  6. At the beginning of each new game, the values of the crytals changes.
